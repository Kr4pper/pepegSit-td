How To Play

You must defend your home from nasty invaders.
They will arrive in waves, make sure to wipe them out.
Build your defense from a variety of towers to stop them.
Select an available tile by using left click and then build one of the available towers using the listed key binding.
Select a tower and press 'X' to sell it for 70% of it's cost.
Use SPACE to toggle fast forward mode, making the game run at 3x speed.
